About me

Before College

I was born in Washington, but grew up in Colorado. As a kid, I played soccer with my brother, and was an active member in band as a trumpet player.

My first coding experience was in 8th grade. We used Scratch, a Block Based visual programming language, used to design video games. I had so much fun, I decided I wanted to code for a living.

During Highschool, I took a couple coding classes before deciding to major in Computer Science in college. I took multiple AP courses, including Computer Science Principles, Computer Science, Statistics, and Calculus BC. I have always had a passion for math, logic, and problem solving.

My Interests

In my freetime, I like to play videogames & Dungeons and Dragons, cooking, digital art, and music.

I've always enjoyed coding things that go hand-in-hand with my interests, so many of my coding projects are related to DND or creating my own video games.

I try to use my creativity and design skills in my everyday life, including my coding projects.
