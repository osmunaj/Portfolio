Involvement

AWC

AWC, short for Association for Women in Computing is also a professional organization, but is dedicated to supporting women and non-binary people in Computing and Technology.

AWC has not only helped me find the importance of equity and diversity, but also a community of people in my field who I can relate to, and enjoy spending time with.

Grace Hopper Conference 2024

I also was able to attend the Grace Hopper Celebration in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, which Celebrates Women in Computing from across the nation. I met so many brilliant women, and it truly inspired me to not let anyone put down my talent as a programmer because of my gender.

oSTEM

oSTEM, short for Out in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathmatics, is a professional organization dedicated to celebrating and promoting diversity for LBGTQ+ people in STEM Fields.

I also had the pleasure of attending the National oSTEM Conference in Boston, Massachusetts in 2022. I learned so much about inclusion, leadership, and community, and the keynote speakers were inspiring. I hope to attend another conference someday, as it was truly a lifechanging event.

As an active member and former treasurer for the club, I recognize the importance of inclusion in the workplace, and am dedicated to making the world of technology a safer place for members of the LGBTQ+ Community.
